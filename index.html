Tandoori: The Flavour of the Clay Oven

Tandoori is a traditional North Indian cooking style where marinated meats, paneer, or vegetables are cooked in a clay oven called a tandoor. The intense heat and open flame give the food a signature smoky flavor and charred texture. Commonly marinated in spiced yogurt blends, tandoori dishes are bold, aromatic, and deeply satisfying.
